Welcome to Skincraft Tattoo studio

Welcome to Skincraft, where we specialize in creating custom, high-quality tattoos that reflect your unique style and personality. Our team is comprised of two exceptionally talented artists, Filip and Masa, who bring a wealth of skill and experience to every tattoo they create. Filip is an expert in all styles of tattoos, from black and gray realism to color realism, geometry, traditional, and Japanese. With an art diploma to his name, he is a true master of his craft and approaches each tattoo with the precision and attention to detail that it deserves. Filip takes pleasure in working closely with clients to understand their vision and transform it into a stunning work of art that they will love for years to come. Masa is a specialist in small and fineline tattoos. Her focus is on intricate details and delicate linework. She has outstanding approach to tattooing that allows her to create designs that are both subtle and striking, making her a go-to choice for anyone looking for a small but meaningful tattoo. At Skincraft, we believe that tattoos should be an expression of your individuality, and we work tirelessly to ensure that each tattoo we create is tailored to your unique style and preferences. Our studio is a welcoming and comfortable space, and our artists are committed to making the tattoo experience as enjoyable as possible for every client. If you're looking for a tattoo studio that combines exceptional talent with a focus on customer satisfaction, look no further than Skincraft. Book your consultation with Filip or Masa today and take the first step towards getting the tattoo of your dreams.
