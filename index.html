Filip Reljic

Tattoo Master

Filip is a skilled tattoo artist with a wealth of experience in his craft, having been tattooing since 2012. He holds an art diploma, which has undoubtedly helped him to hone his artistic skills and develop his unique style. Filip is an expert in all styles of tattoos, including black and gray realism, color realism, geometry, traditional, Japanese, and many more. His versatility as an artist is a true testament to his talent and dedication to his craft. Whether you're looking for a striking black and gray portrait or a vibrant traditional piece, Filip has the skills and expertise to bring your vision to life. With his attention to detail and commitment to quality, you can trust that you'll leave Filip's studio with a tattoo that you'll love for years to come.

Masa Reljic

Tattoo Master

Masa is an exceptional tattoo artist who has began tattooing in 2021 and has since honed her craft to perfection in small and fineline tattoos. She has already established herself as an expert in this highly specialized field. Masa's talent and dedication are evident in every tattoo she creates. She has mastered the use of fine needles to create precise lines and intricate details, resulting in tattoos that are both delicate and stunning. Her knowledge of this specialized area of tattooing has allowed her to develop her own unique style, characterized by its precision and attention to detail.With her expertise and passion, she is sure to create a one-of-a-kind piece of art that you'll treasure for a lifetime.
